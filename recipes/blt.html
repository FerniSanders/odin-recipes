BLT Sandwich

Easy to make sandwhich!!!

A BLT is a delicious sandwhich anyone can make

Ingridients

  • Sandwich Bread Slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. First toast your bread slices
  2. Cook your bacon till crispy
  3. Slice your tomatoe and shredd your lettuce
  4. Assemble your your sandwhich
  5. First add mayo to both slices
  6. Add your lettuce and tomatoe
  7. Add salt and pepper
  8. Add your bacon
  9. Done!!!!!
