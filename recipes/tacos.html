Easy Taco Recipe Anybody Can Do!

This is an easy recipe that does not take much time to prepare

Ingridients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Taco Meat
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Avocado
  • Queso Fresco
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook your meat
  2. After the meat is cooked start heating up your tortillas
  3. Begin to add your cooked meat on your tortillas
  4. Add whatever toppings youd like (Onion, Cilantro, Avocado, Queso Fresco, Salsa)
  5. Enjoy!!!
